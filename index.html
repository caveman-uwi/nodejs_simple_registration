Welcome to Evening Institute!

We are a results driven post-secondary and pre-university educational institution who provides excellent tutoring and study resources delivered by qualified teachers in comfortable and functional facilities.

Education and continuous training are integral tools for upward social mobility, in today's fast pace technology driven work world.

Aims and Objective

Our institution aims to meet the needs of ambitious students, with the various foundation Caribbean Examination Council subjects necessary to matriculate to the tertiary level.
